System Requirements

Minimum System Requirements
OS

Windows 7, XP, and Vista 32
CPU

Intel Pentium 4 class processor or AMD Athlon XP, at 1.5 GHz or faster
Video Card

GeForce FX class or ATI Radeon 9600 with 128MB of dedicated VRAM or better
System Memory

1Gb of RAM or more
Sound

16-bit, DirectX compliant sound card
DirectX

9.0c or better
Drive Space

1.8 GB of HDD space, plus additional for new game content
Other

Broadband Internet Connection

Keyboard, and Mouse with Wheel


Note(s): If your system does not meet or exceed all of the system requirements, Shaiya may still run, but some functionality and performance will be lost. For the best gaming performance, make sure that your computer exceeds the above requirements.

Please be sure that you have closed all other programs and tasks before attempting to play Shaiya.

Some firewall programs will block Shaiya, disallowing the game from accessing the server. If you receive a "Cannot connect to server" or "Cannot change directory.c35" error when trying to play, please make make adjustments to your firewall program(s).
