Shaiya Desire: Episode 5.0

  • Max Level: 70
  • EXP Rate: x50 ( x100 in weekend)
  • KILL Rate: x2
  • Linking Rate: Max Linking Chance is 50%.
  • Lapis: Max Lapis Lv6, Elemental Lapis Lv2.
  • Items: NO Custom Items, NO Knockout Nostrum, Ending Equipments Lv65-69.
  • Free Ending Gears: for PvP areas 15 and 30.
  • Free Buffs: The 30 Days Continues Resurrection, Prevent Item Drop and Eternal Endurance renews every time you login.
  • Stable Economy Server: Gold is highly valuable. You can sell or buy any item you desire with it.
  • Shaiya Points: Can be obtained through PvP Rewards, In-Game at Events and by Donations.
  • Enhanced Server: Server is hosted in a well known DDoS Protected company.
  • Account Security: Website is running under a Secured Socket Layer.
  • Cross Faction Trade: This allow you to trade items with opposite faction in Auction House.
  • Shared Kills on all Raids: Gain same amount of kills with other raids if you are in the same area of PvP.
  • Active, friendly Staffs to keep the game safe and enjoyable for everyone.



Updates are based on players suggestions and what we think is appropriate to enchance your gaming experience.
We fixed most of the well known bugs and exploits in-game, but if you find any, please report it immediately.

Best Regards,
Shaiya Desire Team!

There are 3 types of Lapisia we could use to enchant the weapons:
